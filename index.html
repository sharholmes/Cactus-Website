CARE AND HEALTH FOR
YOUR CACTUS

  • In cold times, add water once a month and during the summer water it when the soil is dry.

  • Use a well-draining potting mix to ensure proper drainage and precent waterlogged roots.

  • Place your cactus in a location that receives plenty of bright indirect sunlight.

  • Water your cactus sparingly, allowing the soil to dry out completely between waterings.

  • Keep your cactus in an environment with consisten temperatures and low humidity.
